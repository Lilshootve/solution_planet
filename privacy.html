How We Use Information

We may share information with vetted suppliers strictly to deliver contracted services (e.g., logistics, rigging, translation), or when required by law. We do not sell your personal data.

We retain data only as long as necessary for operational, legal, and accounting purposes, then delete or anonymize it.

Your Rights

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards. No method is 100% secure; we encourage strong passwords and careful sharing.

International Transfers
